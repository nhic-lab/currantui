- **Foundation** — the design language: tokens, typography, density, and the
  interaction recipe every component follows.
- **Components** — 22 primitives and chrome pieces, each with live variants,
  props tables, and states.
- **Built-in quality gate** — every story runs as a browser render test plus
  an axe accessibility audit in CI. What you see here is verified, not
  aspirational.
