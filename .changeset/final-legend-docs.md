Legend items are now toggle buttons: clicking one hides or shows its series (dimmed strikethrough while hidden), with the state shared between the inline and fullscreen views. Hidden series keep their palette slot, so colors stay bound to their group instead of shifting onto the remaining series. Applies across the bar, line, area, combo, scatter, bubble, pie, donut, meter, and treemap charts.
